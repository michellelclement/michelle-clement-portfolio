Hello! I’m Michelle from London. Thank you for visiting! I am a newly trained, front end developer after completing my diploma in Software Development (Full Stack) with Code Institute.

My background is in brand marketing, creative, and being the person people come to when something doesn’t work. Social scheduling mistakes, image resolution issues, a broken toilet, you name it, I've been asked to fix it.



I spent 11 years in the music industry, 7 of those at the Ministry of Sound, and the last 6 years as a Freelance Marketing Manager across many different sectors including travel, insurance, music, film and events.



Like many others, the start of the pandemic caused much of the freelancing work to wind down. So I took this perfect opportunity of time to dive into learning something I had really wanted to do, web development!



Thanks to Code Institute, I received my diploma in February 2021 with Merit, with my full-stack final project graded a distinction.



After the slight delay to my plans (I found out I was expecting my first baby 1 month into my course, talk about a hard deadline!) it is now time for me to finally pursue my new career in Web Development and hopefully set a good example for my daughter in the process.
